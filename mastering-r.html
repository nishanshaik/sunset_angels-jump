About Me



I am Dr Nishan, your instructor and mentor for the course.

I am a medical doctor and public health professional with over 5 years of working experience in medical, public health, pharmaceutical and consulting sectors. My past work experience include a stint as the health economics specialist for Roche India. I am currently working as an independent research consultant for public health and medical research projects for the past two years.

I did my MBBS from Government Medical College, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala after which I graduated in Master of Public Health from Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai (TISS). It is at TISS that I got trained in research, data, and statistics.

I learnt R while doing my MPH at TISS. R was not a part of the official coursework back then, even so my guides and mentors were supportive and encouraged me to learn the skills. I was able to successfully use the language to perform statistical analysis for all the course requirements in the third and fourth semesters.

Having had the privilege to be mentored and guided by some of the best, I gained confidence to take on projects independently. R and statistics were a part of all of them.

My consulting workflow includes heavy use of R, including for project management, data management, analytics, visualisation and reporting. Working on various real-world projects has allowed me to refine my workflows in R geared towards efficiency and minimalism. Even though I user R extensively, my vocabulary is minimalistic.

I have been guiding and mentoring students and professionals from across the globe in R and analytics for three years now. This course has seen over 150 students in the past 1 year.

I am based out of Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala where I live with my family.
